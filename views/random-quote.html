Random Quote is a chrome plugin designed to show you interesting quotes throughout your day while online. Once Random Quote is installed, there will be an interesting quote instead of a blank page when you open a new tab.


This is a simple and fun way to see interesting quotes throughout the day without going out of your way to look them up.
